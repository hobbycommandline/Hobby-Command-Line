This is the AI model that is fed into the Vosk API that is the
brain behind translating your voice into text locally. Like Vosk API,
the model is also Apache 2.0. Downloaded from https://alphacephei.com/vosk/models

Notes from the website:
Lightweight wideband model for Android and RPi
Word error rate/Speed:
9.85 (librispeech test-clean) 10.38 (tedlium)
